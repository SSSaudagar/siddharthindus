WELCOME TO SIDDHARTH INDUSTRIES

SiddharthIndustries is the pioneer and the leading Metal Extrusion Press manufacturing company in India, engaged in the manufacturing of Extrusion Press and Plants for Aluminium, Copper, Brass, Precious Metal and special purpose equipment etc.

SiddharthIndustries with an experience of more than 30 years has acquired the technology and expertise to offer optimal solutions for extrusion. The company has never compromised on Quality & Values, while achieving the desired goals and objectives.

SIDDHARTH INDUSTRIES manufactures Extrusion Presses and Complete Automatic Plants ranging from 360 MT to 10000 MT capacity. The clientele of the company is spread over American, European and Asian continents.

Using the latest and most modern technologies, SIDDHARTH INDUSTRIES supplies its customers with more than just machines - we offer solutions for the State of the Art production facilities. Latest technologies for design, precise machining facilities for manufacture and innovative automation for control gives SIDDHARTH INDUSTRIES a cutting edge to meet the demands of today's Engineering world.
