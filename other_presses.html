OTHER HYDRAULIC PRESSES & POWER PACKS

SIDDHARTH INDUSTRIES, with its unique specialization, enviable state of the art technology and unwavering focus on quality, has made a mark in the International market.

SIDDHARTH INDUSTRIES has also forged successfully in the Indian market supplying machinery to a host of reputed companies like Bajaj Auto Ltd., Larson and Toubro, TVS Group, Amalgamation Group, Rane Luk & severla LPG Cylinder manufacturers.

Metal Pressing & drawing presses

SIDDHARTH INDUSTRIES developed high speed productive presses for Metal Pressing, Metal Drawing, Straightening etc. suitable for Automobile components manufacturers, L.P.G. Gas cylinder manufacturers, Rubber Component manufacturers etc.

The presses are designed by using finite element analysis to meet the acute requirements of the users.

The Deep Drawing Presses are manufactured with Four pre Stretched Tie Rods, Compressed Column Design & Hardened Rams.

The presses are manufactured as per Japanese Industries Standards [JIS-B 6403-1973] The manufacturing range of these presses is of 100 M.T. to 2000 M.T.

'C' Frame Presses

The 'C' Frame Presses are designed for the minimum angular deflection for the purpose of straightening, embossing and for different kind of assemblies.

The safety guards and P.L.C. can be provided on specific requirements.

The manufacturing range is from 25 M.T. to 500 M.T.

Special purpose machines and Other products


The Special Purpose Machines(SPM) are manufactured and supplied on customer's specific requirements.


HYDRAULIC POWER PACKS for special application and press applications, Quenching Machines cylinders, Powder Compacting Presses etc. All these presses and our state of the art EXTRUSION PRESSES are manufactured with Ultra Modern FACILITIES at SIDDHARTH INDUSTRIES.
