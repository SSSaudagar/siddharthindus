Our Company

SIDDHARTH INDUSTRIES is a professionally managed Engineering Company

The company is actively involved in international business in the field of Hydraulic Presses and equipments since 1980. Siddharth Hydraulic Presses cover the widest range of applications such as:

  • Metal Extrusion (Nonferrous for Aluminium, Copper, Brass, Precious metals and their alloys.)
  • Metal Pressing.
  • Compression Moulding (Rubber Application)
  • Coining
  • Special Purpose Equipments

Siddharth's Experience

Need based, continuous product improvement is the main feature of nearly two decades of Siddharth's experience. Constant learning and indepth study of requirements of extrusion presses have enabled Engineers at Siddharth to meet the varying demands of different Clients.

The immense knowledge generated over past several years on the Extrusion Industry aids the company in educating the new comers and in guiding them to comfortably execute their projects.

The reliability and consistent performance of presses supplied have assisted Siddharth Industries in booking successful repeat orders from many satisfied customers.

Performance and Quality

With the vast experience generated in the past several years and constant endeavour to improve upon the Performance and the Quality of its Hydraulic Presses, the Company has established itself as a Pioneer leading manufacturer in Indian subcontinent.

Office and Manufacturing

Office and Manufacturing unit with advanced inhouse FACILITIES, located at BELGAUM in Southern India is easily accessible from major places like Mumbai, Bangalore and Goa. Every equipment manufactured passes through several quality checks, right from raw materials to the finished components, thus ensuring trouble-free operations at the User's end.

Having proved over time for their quality and performance SIDDHARTH PRESSES are well accepted and appreciated by our customers both in the domestic and International market.
